SwithcBlades logo

Switchblades poster



Click anywhere to exit

This is a logo for a graduation project in technology class. The project was a quick switching system for rollerblades. The logo below the name is a representation of the mechanism that locked it into place. The logo has an overall slight tilt to indicate speed, while the name is a play on words, as the product served the purpose of switching rollerblade frames out fast.
